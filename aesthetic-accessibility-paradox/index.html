Aesthetic-Accessibility Paradox

In a thread on Twitter , Cat Noone explains why there is no such thing.

Finding a middle ground between what is aesthetically pleasing and what is simultaneously usable by everyone is your job as a designer.

  • Form follows function every time
  • Accessibility about is permanent, temporal and situational scenarios (e.g. one arm, arm injury or new parent)
